The Kinobody Roadmap

Guide ▶

All programs share ~80% of the same structure. The remaining 20% changes based on your goal. Get lean first, then build.

Step 1: Get Lean

Target waist = ~44-45% of height (6'0" = ~32" waist). If 3" above target, that's ~15 lbs to lose (~5 lbs/inch).

The Progression

PhaseProgramGoalWhen
1Warrior Movie Star CutCutStarting point. Deficit + 10k steps. Until abs are sharp.
2Greek God Strength AcceleratorLean BulkOnce lean. Rapid strength. Run until progress slows.
3Greek God Physique MasteryLean BulkAfter Accelerator. Refine proportions. Maintenance/surplus.
4Specialization (Man of Steel)Lean BulkSculpt lagging areas. Not for cutting.
5Ripped ArtisteMaintainLifestyle phase. Stay sharp year-round.

Key Principles

  • Train for strength, not exhaustion
  • Keep one rep in the tank on key lifts
  • Stick with each program 2-4 months minimum
  • Plateau? Switch to a close exercise variation for 3-4 weeks
  • Cutting = Warrior or Ripped Artiste
  • Building = Greek God or Specialization
