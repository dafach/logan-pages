Variable Sets Guide

Use HIGHER set count (2-3 or 1-2 → higher) if:

  • Not pressed for time
  • Training less often (Every Other or 3x/week)
  • Don't need to reduce rest periods to fit all volume

Use LOWER set count if:

  • Pressed for time
  • Training more often (4x/week)
  • Would need to reduce rest periods to fit all work

Intensity Tips

  • Trust your RIR? Stop at 0-1 RIR as prescribed
  • Not confident? Take sets to ~0 RIR/failure to avoid sandbagging
  • Safety first: Don't take 50/50 reps (unsure reps) on exercises like Hack Squats — avoid susceptible positions

General Dean Turner Principles

Philosophy▶
  • All working sets @ 0-1 RIR (1 RIR preferred for safety)
  • If you can't gauge RIR, just go to failure — but never take 50/50 reps on compounds
  • 3+ min rest between working sets (2 min absolute floor)
  • 60-90 sec rest between warm-up sets
  • Low volume, high intensity, progressive overload
  • Machine-heavy for safety at high intensity
  • 5-10 rep range for most movements, 6-15 for isolation
